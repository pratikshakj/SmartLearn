Do you have an impressive personality or possess extremely good communication skills? Then hospitality is a good career choice for you. Hospitality management as a profession requires one to possess strong interpersonal communication skills, be meticulous and have business sense. The first step in making a career in the field of hospitality is to pursue a course from a good college and one of the most popular programmes pursued by students right after their Class 12 in this field is Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM). BHM is an undergraduate course which spans for a duration of three years. However, some colleges such as Welcomgroup Graduate School of Hotel Administration offer it as a four-year degree programme. As part of a BHM course, candidates are taught about different facets of the hospitality industry. Thus, in a BHM course aspirants are taught food and beverage preparation, housekeeping operations, front office operations, computer applications, business communication, accommodation operation, corporate finance, sales, marketing, public relations etc. BHM course is known to have theory as well as practical subjects. The BHM course also emphasises on developing and enhancing personal skills of an aspirant as a good personality is a must in this career path. Apart from this, the BHM course also educates students on aspects such as hygiene, food safety as well as nutrition that is in tune with international standards.

Required Skillset for BHM

To make a career in the field of hotel management candidates need to possess a service minded approach. Some key skills that an aspirant should possess when they consider pursuing a BHM course are listed below: , Ability to adjust in a crowd/different circumstances , Outgoing personality , Pleasant personality , Polite demeanour , Flair for creativity , Customer-oriented approach , Responsible , Willingness to work long and odd hours ,Discipline , Ability to be committed and dedicated to a task , Confidence , Good listener

BHM Eligibility Criteria

Students can pursue BHM course right after their Class 12. For most colleges the eligibility criteria to pursue a BHM course is that the candidate should have cleared his 10+2 from a recognised board. However, other colleges have specific requirement that candidates need to fulfill in order to pursue a BHM course which include that aspirants should have secured at least 50 per cent aggregate marks in their Class 12 exams or should have studied English at their Class 12 level and secured passing marks in the subject. Some hotel management colleges also set age limit for candidates who can apply for and secure admission in BHM course offered by them. These colleges haved fixed 22 years as the upper age limit for the students belonging to General or Other Backward Classes (OBC) category and 25 years for Scheduled Caste (SC) and Scheduled Tribes (ST) category.

Course Curriculum for BHM

The subjects taught in BHM course vary from college to college. Also, the BHM course is offered at some colleges as a three-year programme while at some it is a four-year course. Popular colleges that offer hospitality management course offer some or all the subjects that are listed below: , Introduction to Hospitality & Tourism Industry , Food & Beverage Service , Food Production , Front Office Operations , Business Communication , Accommodation Operation , Bakery , Hospitality Accounting , Accommodation Management , Hospitality Information Systems , Food Science & Nutrition , Corporate Finance , Global Food Trends , Food Safety Management Systems ,Human Resources Management , Travel & Tourism , Hospitality Facility Planning , Hospitality Marketing , Indian Heritage & Culture , Business Law and Ethics , Environment Sustainability , Entrepreneurship Development , Foreign Language (any one) , English Apart from the above mentioned theory subjects, candidates who pursue BHM course, also need to attempt and clear practical papers in subjects such as Food Production, Front Office, Food & Beverage Service and House Keeping. As part of BHM course candidates need to mandatorily undergo industrial training of few months.
