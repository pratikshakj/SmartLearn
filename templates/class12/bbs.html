BBS (Bachelor of Business Studies) course is a 3 year undergraduate programme that deals with the business management orbit. This course is a combination of all marketing, financial, economic and human resources related aspects of the economy. To pursue this course you need to possess a minimum of 55%-60% score in your higher secondary class and have to clear the common entrance exam. To get admission in the BBS course offered by Delhi University colleges , you need to appear for the DU JAT examination. Moreover, this course cannot be opted from distance learning. BBS syllabus comprises subjects like Financial Accounting, Macroeconomics, Principles of Marketing, Organizational Behaviour and alike.

BBS Course: Admission Process

You can apply for BBS course from both online and offline mode. The admission process for the same varies from college to college. BBS admissions are based on entrance exam only. Earlier BBS admissions i.e. before 2015 followed the preocedure of first entrance exam and then group discussion and personal interview. As per the new policies, now the admissions are offered on the basis of 12th standard and the entrance exam score. Few steps are given below to help you with the admission process: You either have to visit the official website of the College or the Administration office of the college to avail the application forms for admission in BBS course. Normally, admission forms are available at the college websites now-a-days for students convenience. Now you need to fill the application form as directed and then upload the relevant and asked documents. These documents involves attested X th and XII th scorecards, caste certificate (if asked), passport size photographs, scanned signature and identity proof. Now the final step is to pay the application fee online through payment portals. Then at a notified date you have to appear for the BBS entrance examination. Once the results of the entrance exams will be announced or if the admission of any college is merit based and they release their merit list, selected candidates have to appear for the further documentation and counselling process at the notified place. Entrance Exams conducted by different universities are also followed by the further rounds of group discussion and personal Interview. Depending upon the cutoff or the score, admissions will be provided in this course.

BBS Course: Eligibility Criteria

One should possess a minimum of 60% in 10+2 in any relevant stream with English as a mandatory subject. There’s a 5% relaxation for SC, ST and Physically disabled categories. The qualifying percentage of 10+2 varies from institute to institute. Minimum age to join this BBS course is 18 years and there is no such upper age limit specification. To pursue this course you need to appear for the college or university level entrance exam too, which is further followed by the group discussion and personal interview round.

BBS Course: Entrance Exam

Many of the colleges offering BBS have their own entrance examinations. If you wish to pursue BBS course from a Delhi University college then you have to appear for DU-JAT entrance test. DU JAT: Delhi University Joint Admission Test (DU JAT) is an entrance test conducted by university of delhi to offer admission in courses like BBS/BMS/BBE/BFIA. This exam is conducted once in a year in the month of June. How to prepare for Entrance Examination?

BBS entrance examinations basically covers 4 sections: ,Quantitative Ability ,General and Business Awareness ,General English ,General intelligence and Reasoning

Points to keep in mind while preparing for entrance exams

As there are 4 sections in the entrance examination of bachelor of business studies and each of them hold their own importance. The difficulty level of each of the section changes by every. So, you should give equal attention to each subject and prepare seperate notes register of each of them. You can also practice previous year sample papers or question papers. They are easily available on either the college website or in the form of books at book shops. By practicing these papers you will be able to recognise the changing pattern of examination. You can also appear for online mock tests (if available). Few of these websites have this feature for students to practice any particular section mock test at a time. To enhance your general and business awareness section, you should read newspapers, books or journals on daily basis. These sources will help you to get hold on all national and international happenings in the world. Follow the time table you have made, so that, you will be ale to finish the syllabus on time and get spare time for revision purpose.

BBS Course: What is About?

BBS course full form is Bachelor of Business Studies course. This course covers the subjects related to business and finance. They help in enhancing the analytical, intellectual and interpersonal skills of an aspirant. BBS course is often related to BBA course, but both of these courses are different from each other. BBS focuses more on the real life management issues and scenarios. This course embarks the entrepreneurial skills in an aspirant. This course curriculum comprises both theory and practical subjects. It trains you in the form of internships, industrial visits, presentations and case projects.
